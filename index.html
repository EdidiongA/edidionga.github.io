About Me

I have a Bachelor of Science (BSc) degree in Computer Science from Babcock University, Nigeria which was obtained in 2008. I also hold a Master of Science in Computer Science (MSCS) degree from the Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta, Georgia (2018 to 2024). I hold a number of IT certifications namely: CompTIA Network+ certification (2007), Microsoft Certified Professional (2008), Microsoft Certified Technology Specialist (Business Desktop Deployment) (2009), MCSA – Microsoft Certified Solutions Associate (Windows Server 2012) (2015), Cisco Certified Network Associate (2015), Cisco Certified Network Professional (2015) and Social/Behavioral Research Investigators and Key Personnel (CITI Program, A Division of BRANY) (2019). I am a member of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) and the International Society of Automation (ISA). I am currently the Assistant Systems Administrator at the West African Institute for Financial and Economic Management (WAIFEM) (2011 to date) and I have worked at the Shell Petroleum Development Company Ltd as a Instrumentation Engineer under the NNPC/Nigerian Content Development and Monitoring Board from 2010 to 2011. I was selected to participate in the Google Computer Science Research Mentorship programme (September to December 2022) and I am a recipient of the National Information Technology Development Agency (NITDA)/Coursera scholarship program for training in User Interface/User Experience development and design and Entrepreneurship (June to December 2023). I have carried out a number of notable projects in the following areas - Android application development, database application development, UI/UX design, penetration testing, data science and artificial intelligence/machine learning. Latest Projects

AI Fairness and Bias (using Python)

The use of machine learning regression classifier models and the AI Fairness 360 toolkit to calculate Statistical Parity Difference and Disparate Impact fairness metrics (fairness measures) for fairness and bias in the Taiwan Credit allocations dataset (dataset obtained from the UC Urvine Machine Learning repository) - April 2023.

AI/ML Word2Vec and FacialRecognition(using Python)

The application of Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning algorithms to two applications – Google’s deep learning Word2vec word embeddings system and a facial recognition system using the UTK dataset. The pre-trained Word2vec model (based on the Google News dataset) was obtained from Google Code. The UTK dataset was obtained from UTKFace - March 2023

Data Analysis on Toxicity(using Python)

Data and inferential statistical analysis using Python on a natural language classifier built to identify toxicity in comments from Wikipedia Talk Pages - March 2023

Data and Statistical Analysis (using Python)

Data and Statistical Analysis, using Python, on deaths that occur in custody or during the process of arrest in California (dataset source - https://openjustice.doj.ca.gov/data) - February 2023

Facebook Ad Info Retrieval (using Python)

The use of a natural langauge processing transformer model (Wikipedia transformer model) to retrieve Facebook ad information from my personal Facebook profile in certain categories - January 2023

Penetration testing with GoPhish

A penetration testing campaign developed using GoPhish (an email phishing platform), set up on a remote Linux VPS complete with domain hosting, a digital certificate (for the domain) and email authentication - September 2022.

GameSwap

a bartering database web application created with Angular/Node.js (frontend), Java and MySQL (backend) for trading board games, video game systems, and other home entertainment gaming items - April 2022.

JobCompare

Android application which compares different jobs or job offers based on an algorithm that calculates the weights of several aspects of each job such as job title, company name, location, overall living costs, commute time, yearly salary and yearly bonus - August 2020.
